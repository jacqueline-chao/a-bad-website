Violet:

Violet is a deep and beautiful color! It is also the color of royalty.

Blue:

Blue can come in so many different shades, and all of them are beautiful! Both the sky and the sea are blue.

Green:

Green is a very calming color. When people think of nature, they typically think of the color green as well. It can be a deep forest green or a funky neon green!

Yellow:

Yellow reminds me of sunshine. If metallic and of a deeper shade, it could be described as gold. In some cultures, yellow is a special color, connotating wealth, power, or fortune.

Orange:

Orange is a very energetic color. It invokes physical activity and determination for me. It's a solid color!

Red:

Red is also a very energetic color. While it may be associated with aggression, I think energy and drive would be more accurate! The boldness of red is what makes it my favorite color.
